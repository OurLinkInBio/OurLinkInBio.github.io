M.A.R.V.I.S

About!

M.A.R.V.I.S stands for, Medical Android for Rescue & Vital Intervention Services. And is to be a science fiction interpretation of what medical produces could potentially look like within the future. Whilst also being an immersive, interactive and simulating experience for players to enjoy. Within MARVIS, you portray a futuristic android who is apart of the Xelerii race.

Game Design Document

Every game requires one of these. These documents, for those who aren't aware are for development teams to know the concept and the breakdown of game systems, a generalisation of features, mechanics, assets, sounds and more of what is mainly required for the game to function. The game design document, otherwise known as a GDD was created fairly quickly and contains the base concept of the game, as well as some minor and simplistic mechanics and features.


Technical Design Document

The technical design document otherwise known as a TDD is more or less for the programmers and designers use, it allows the more technical side of the project development the ability to plan out how they would created the features and systems.

Our TDD is still in production and has not yet receach a standard to which we can start production with. We will keep you posted on this.
